Smooth Sweet Tea

Description

Sweet tea is a refreshing beverage that is perfect for hot summer days. This classic Southern drink is simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pinch backing soda
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 6 tea bags
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 6 cups cool water

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Sprinkle a pinch of backing soda into a 64-ounce, heat-proof glass pitcher. Pour in boiling water and add tea bags. Cover and allow to steep for 15min.
  3. Remove tea bags and discard; stir in sugar until dissolved. Pour in cool water; refrigerate until cold, about 3h
  4. Enjoy over ice on a hot day!
